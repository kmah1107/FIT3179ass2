Covid-19 in India

This page shows the extent of the spread of Covid-19 in India. India currently has the highest number of confirmed cases in Asia and the second highest number of cases in the worl after the United States. With the total confirmed cases are calculated to be around 8.23 million and still increasing every single day. With more than 1 lakh deaths and 7 million recovered. Despite the strict lockdown in the entire nation India has not been able to defeat this disease.
